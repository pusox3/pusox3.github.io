Who We Are

Established in 1994, the Philippine United Student Organization (PUSO) is a Filipino cultural-interest organization at Stony Brook University. We consist of three dance teams, PUSO Cultural, PUSO Ballroom and PUSO Modern, that perform and compete at events both on and off campus year round.

We fulfill our mission statement by hosting events such as an Ate/Kuya mentoring program, Sayaw; Stony Brook’s largest dance competition, and PUSOfest; a showcase of our general body members and a celebration of the year’s accomplishments.

Anyone is welcome to join! General Body Meetings are in Melville Library E4320 on Mondays from 8PM - 9 PM

Our Mission Statement

To create a social environment in which Filipino culture is advocated, discussed and celebrated
