Who We Are

The Philippine United Student Organization, PUSO, is a Filipino cultural-interest club located at Stony Brook University. Anyone is welcome to join. We hold a multitude of events on campus to bring awareness to Filipino culture, and we also have 3 dance teams, one of which promotes traditional Filipino dances.

Our Mission Statement

To create a social environment in which Filipino culture is advocated, discussed and celebrated
